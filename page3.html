We are greatly pleased that you have decided to undertake your middle cadre health studies at our Bulongwa health Sciences Institute or you are considering to do so. This website describes the range of our courses and activities done at our institute which will give you essence of acquiring necessary knowledge, skills and behaviors required to deliver effective and efficiency health services to the community.

Bulongwa Health Sciences Institute is a faith based community of students and staff dedicated to bring about the best desired outcome of learning. The aim of this institute is to produce committed and competent health care workers, who will be promoting health, providing health services to persons in need and preventing health problems of the community at any setting.

The community of this Institute believes that the quality of graduates is a result of interaction between students, staff and other members of the community around the Institute.
