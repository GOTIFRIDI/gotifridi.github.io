Admission to the Institute

The Institute is currently offering Diploma Course in Clinical Dentistry and Certificate in Nursing and midwifery.
Application
Application for the year 2017/2018 academic year is through the Institute. An application fee of 30,000/= tsh is paid through bank-BULONGWA HEALTH SCIENCES INSTITUTE ,ACC.NO.0150226058000 CRDB OR 60403500081 NMB. Then application form is filled and submitted to the Institute.

Ordinary Diploma in Clinical Medicine
A pass in ordinary level secondary school for the sciences subjects of Biology C, Chemistry C and Physics D, English D and Mathematics D.

Certificate in Clinical Assistants
A pass in Biology D, Chemistry D, Physics D, Mathematics D and English D.

Ordinary Diploma in Clinical Dentistry
A pass in ordinary level secondary school for the sciences subjects of Biology C, Chemistry C and Physics D will be eligible. Pass in Mathematics and English is added advantage

Certificate in Nursing And Midwfery
A pass in Biology C, Chemistry C, Physics D, and English D. Also a pass in Mathematics is an added Advantage

Certificate in Community Health Worker
A pass in four subjects including BIOLOGY, EXCEPT RELIGIOUS SUBJECTS.

Students Services

 Food and Accomodation

Food and accommodation are available within the Institute campus. Contribution for this service is stipulated in the joining instruction.

No student is allowed to cook in the Dormitory.

 Health Services

The Institute offers common health services which are available at Bulongwa Hospital.

 Sports And Recreation

Students are fully participating and sharing sports with other groups in the community around the Institute.

The Institute has Sports ground for students.

In the assembly hall there is a Television.

Dances are not allowed by the Institute.

The Ministry of social welfare is arranging the schedules.

This service is controlled by students themselves in collaboration with Social Welfare Teacher.

 Religious Activities

The Church and the Pastor is available in the Hospital premises. Other Churches are also available within the community around the Institute.

We respect individuals regerdless of their religion.
